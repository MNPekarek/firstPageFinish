About

Our story, from humble beginnings to innovation-driven growth.

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    In our early days, we started as a small team of passionate innovators working from a modest office. Driven by creativity and determination, we focused on delivering impactful solutions for our first clients.

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    With growing demand for our services, we officially launched our agency, expanding our team and offering specialized services. This marked the beginning of our journey to establish long-term partnerships with clients worldwide.

  • December 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    LBy 2015, we transformed into a full-service digital agency, offering comprehensive solutions in web development, design, and marketing. This transition enabled us to tackle larger, more ambitious projects.

  • July 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    Amid a rapidly changing industry, we expanded our services to include e-commerce platforms and advanced security solutions. This phase strengthened our position as a leader in the digital space.
